Sequence Narration Visuals Audio Description
0 [INTRO MUSIC] [TITLE] Web Accessibility Perspectives - Text to Speech [before narration:] Web accessibility perspectives
1 [TEXT TO SPEECH VOICE]
Some people can't see text on the screen.		 A man sitting on a sofa using a laptop computer.
[TEXT TO SPEECH VOICE]
Fortunately computers can convert text to speech.		 [TEXT ON SCREEN WITH WORD-HIGHLIGHTING]
Fortunately computers can convert text to speech.		 [after narration (and after text-to-speech finishes):] A man is using a laptop with the text on the screen being highlighted as it is spoken.
2 [Regular narration]
It’s technology that many people who are blind have been relying on for years.		 The man is using text to speech. Pull out to reveal a service dog next to him. We realize the man using the computer is blind. [after narration:] A service dog is next to the man; he may be blind.
3 But it’s also important for many people with dyslexia... Cut to a woman without visible disability with headphones on, using text to speech on a tablet. [after narration:] A woman is reading on a tablet screen and listening with headphones.
4 ...and very useful for people with difficulty reading text. We see someone take off their glasses and sit comfortably, listening to a news article on a tablet being read to them. [after narration:] A man removes his glasses and listens to a news article on a tablet.
5 As well as some people who just like to multi-task. Cut to a person listening to a magazine article as they do the washing. [after narration:] A man listens to a magazine article as they do the washing.
6 But for this to work, websites and apps have to be properly coded. Show the woman from earlier with headphones on trying to use text to speech on a website and it is not working. [after narration:] A website is not properly working for the woman with headphones; she is frustrated.
7 Which has the added benefit of helping search engines index website contents better! Continue above. [after narration:] The man with service dog is using his mobile device outside with bone conduction headphones.
8 Web accessibility: essential for some, useful for all. (with text to speech audible in the background) Different people from earlier using their devices and listening. [after narration:] Different people from earlier are shown using text to speech.
