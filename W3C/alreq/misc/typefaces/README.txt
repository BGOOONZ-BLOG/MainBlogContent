IR-Mitra and Source Sans Pro are used for preparation of images of the document.

* IR-Mitra is a version of the Mitra typeface that was prepared and freely distributed by Iran Supreme Council of Information And Communication Technology (SCICT) as part of a collection named “Standard Iranian Fonts” and is not included here.
* Source Sans Pro is distributed under SIL Open Font License Version 1.1 and is available at https://github.com/adobe-fonts/source-serif-pro.
