    [karlgroves] One of the things to keep in mind about 508 is that its purpose is to describe the US Federal Government’s approach toward eliminating discrimination against PWDs in the procurement and deployment of ICT.

    IOW it defines how to buy ICT that is accessible and defines what standards are used to define what “accessible” means.

    Incorporating by Reference allows them to point to existing independently developed standards, rather than defining their own standards.
