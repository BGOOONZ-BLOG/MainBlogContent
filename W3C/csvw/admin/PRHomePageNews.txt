[Sent to Comm on the 16th of November]

Subject: [DRAFT HPN] Proposed Recommendations published for CSV on the Web.

----------------------------

<p>The <a href="https://www.w3.org/2013/csvw/wiki/Main_Page">CSV on the Web Working Group</a> has just published a Proposed Recommendation for four documents, namely:</p>

<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.w3.org/TR/2015/PR-tabular-data-model-20151117/">Model for Tabular Data and Metadata on the Web</a>—an abstract model for tabular data, and how to locate metadata that enables users to better understand what the data holds; this specification also contains non-normative guidance on how to parse CSV files</li>
<li><a href="http://www.w3.org/TR/2015/PR-tabular-metadata-20151117/">Metadata Vocabulary for Tabular Data</a>—a JSON-based format for expressing metadata about tabular data to inform validation, conversion, display and data entry for tabular data</li>
<li><a href="http://www.w3.org/TR/2015/PR-csv2json-20151117/">Generating JSON from Tabular Data on the Web</a>—how to convert tabular data into JSON</li>
<li><a href="http://www.w3.org/TR/2015/PR-csv2rdf-20151117/">Generating RDF from Tabular Data on the Web</a>—how to convert tabular data into RDF</li>
</ul>

<p>Comments are welcome until the 15th of December, 2015</p>
