    [deconspray] @ooomph @johan is spot on. Building awareness is demonstrating the situations where design and development impact everyone, including individuals with disabilities. Real people using the products, apps, websites can shock, wow and inspire folks. There may be a few stragglers, but they will come around eventually through persistence and vigilance. Sometimes it also helps so how things that benefit people with disabilities also benefit able-bodied folks. Scenarios like captions on a TV in a gym, or a worker struggling to use a mobile app outdoors in the sun. The scenario and environment can be disabling as well. Good luck in your endeavor.
