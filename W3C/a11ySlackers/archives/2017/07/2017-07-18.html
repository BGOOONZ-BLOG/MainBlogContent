  37. [marcysutton] You do need the aria attributes if there's no actual text in there. aria-label will expose text on the link to assistive technology users, and aria-hidden will prevent the title from being read too...since that's only for mouse users.
  38. [marcysutton] the aria-hidden will also prevent any font glyphs from being read aloud (or at least attempted), since those are generally for visual presentation only.
  39. [sandecorbett] Thanks, @marcysutton! I’ll share this with the developer. I’m not sure if we can change the outer div to an anchor before release, but we can at least look at it for the next iteration.
