This Page is Out of Date

On 15 October 2016 the Internationalization WG switched to using the Github Projects feature to track reviews.

The new RADAR page is located at: 

https://github.com/w3c/i18n-activity/projects/1

This page was used to summarize specifications that the Internationalization Working Group was aware and needing to be reviewed for internationalization-related considerations. Please refer to the instructions for how to raise and track issues.

The Internationalization community is invited to participate in this process. By contributing reviews and review comments, you can help ensure that W3C specifications and documents from other standards bodies and groups that the Internationalization Working Group has reviewed are truly "global ready". Help us advance our mission of making the World Wide Web truly World-wide!
