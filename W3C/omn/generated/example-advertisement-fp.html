FUSECO Playgroundni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: http://fuseco.fokus.fraunhofer.de/ontology#FusecoPlayground

is defined by
http://www.fuseco-playground.org

The Future Seamless Communication (FUSECO) Playground - located in Berlin - is a pioneering reference testbed, integrating various state of the art wireless broadband networks into a 3GPP Evolved Packet Core (EPC) prototype platform, allowing the rapid validation of new networking paradigms, and prototyping of innovative Future Internet and smart city applications.

belongs to
ontology:Testbedc
