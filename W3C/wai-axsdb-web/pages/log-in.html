Why Login?

Anyone can create a W3C Account and use their account to login into this database. Logged-in users are provided with additional features including:

  • Saving testing profiles with information on the web browsers and assistive technlogy used;
  • Options to receive notifications when relevant test results and comments are contributed;
  • Overview of the test cases, test results, and comments they have contributed to the database;
  • Contributions they make to the database are publicly associated with their W3C Account.
