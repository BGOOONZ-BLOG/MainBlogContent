Mission

Mission: To foster a community of users of the Internationalization Tag Set (ITS) Version 1.0 and ITS 2.0, by promoting its adoption within and outside of W3C, and gathering information on its further development.

The Internationalization Tag Set Interest Group is part of the W3C Internationalization Activity.

For more details, see the Interest Group Charter. Note that the scope of the charter currently is only ITS 1.0. An update to include ITS 2.0 is under consideration.

How to Join

  • Subscribe to the public-i18n-its-ig@w3.org mailing list ( public archive).
  • Contribute to the ITS IG wiki. This is an open forum aiming at gathering usage scenarios, infos about implementations and new issues related to ITS 1.0 and ITS 2.0.
