Appendix: Image Descriptions for Accessibilty Requirements for People with Low Vision

2.4.5 Color Vision

Below is a text description of the Figures 4, 5 and & 6 in the Low Vision Requirements document. These figures show that colors that are different for people with full color perception may appear to be the same color for different types of color blindness.

The Position column refers to the clock position in the color wheel. The Gray values are the Brightness levels from Hue, Saturation and Brightness settings.
